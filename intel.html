The basics

So what exactly is Rialo?

Rialo is a decentralized, high-performance blockchain — a full-stack programmable network built to bring Web3 into everyday life. That phrase "full-stack" is doing real work here.

It means everything a modern app needs — live data feeds, automatic execution, cross-chain movement, and privacy-first UX — is built directly into the protocol itself. Not assembled from separate tools. Not patched on from outside. Built in from day one.

The result is apps that feel like real software. You sign in with email or phone. There's no wallet to set up, no seed phrase to write down, no approval pop-up asking you to sign something cryptic. The chain handles the complexity invisibly.

It's built by Subzero Labs, founded by engineers who previously built Sui at Mysten Labs. They've been deep inside this problem before. Rialo is their answer to everything they saw was still missing.

The edge

What makes Rialo different?

Six things the team built natively into the protocol. Each one sounds simple. Together, they change what's actually possible to build.

01

Real World Apps

Web calls, event triggers, and cross-chain flows are built directly into the protocol. Developers write logic — the infrastructure does the rest, without middleware or bots holding things together.

02

Real Time Data

Native pricing feeds with stable, predictable costs. Live market data, credit scores, and external API responses flow directly into smart contracts. No oracle required. No third-party dependency.

03

Sub-Second Reactions

Rialo is event-driven. When a condition is met — a price moves, a timer fires, a message lands — the chain reacts on its own. No gas wars. No polling. No waiting for someone to push a button.

04

No Bridges Needed

Data and assets move across chains and apps natively. Smarter pricing, fairer swaps, faster settlements — all without wrappers, intermediaries, or the security risks that come with bridge architecture.

05

Privacy First UX

Encrypted messages to email, phone, and device. Native 2FA, scheduled transactions, and social login are all built in. Users interact with Rialo apps the way they already interact with everything else online.

06

Ergonomic By Design

Async/await, randomness, and RISC-V architecture so developers write actual product logic — not infrastructure scaffolding. The stack gets out of the way and lets builders build.

Co-Founder & CEO

Ade's career is a study in building things that matter at scale. At Netflix, he worked on distributed infrastructure for one of the world's most demanding platforms. At AMD, he developed advanced microprocessor technology that pushed the boundaries of what hardware could do.

At Mysten Labs, he was a core contributor to the Sui network — where he gained firsthand experience building a high-performance L1 blockchain from the ground up. That experience is the direct foundation of what Rialo runs on.
